Riyadh Villa Solar Project

Location: Riyadh, KSA Category: Residential Completed: Sep 2024

The Challenge

The homeowner, Mr. Ahmed, was facing frequent power outages that disrupted his family's daily life and work-from-home schedule. He also wanted to maximize the benefit from his rooftop solar panels and reduce his carbon footprint.

The Solution

MEGA POWER designed and installed a comprehensive energy solution featuring:

  • One MEGA POWER MP-H15 (15.36 kWh) battery unit.
  • Seamless integration with the existing 10kW solar PV system.
  • Smart configuration to prioritize self-consumption of solar energy and provide instant, automatic backup power.

The Result

The system now provides over 95% energy independence for the family. Mr. Ahmed enjoys uninterrupted power and has seen a reduction of over 80% in his monthly electricity bills.
