Our MP-H5 is the cornerstone of home energy independence, offering a perfect balance of capacity, safety, and longevity. Ideal for storing solar energy and ensuring your home stays powered during outages.

Product Description

The MEGA POWER MP-H5 represents the next generation of home energy storage. Engineered for reliability and performance, it integrates seamlessly with new or existing solar panel systems. Its sleek, stackable design allows for easy installation and scalability, meaning you can expand your energy storage capacity as your needs grow.

Key Features:

  • High-Efficiency LiFePO4 Cells: Provides longer lifespan and superior safety compared to traditional batteries.
  • Advanced BMS: Our integrated Battery Management System protects against overcharging, over-discharging, and extreme temperatures.
  • Scalable Architecture: Start with one unit and add up to 15 more in parallel for a massive storage capacity. For more details, contact our support team.
